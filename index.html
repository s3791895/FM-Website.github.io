Project Idea

An automated Cooking Assistance System (CAS) that will help with home cooking. The CAS will provide recipes from all over the web and information on request such as conversions from Fahrenheit to Celsius. A fully integrated system will automate the menial tasks involved in cooking many recipes. Remote functionality will be available to allow users to begin and control cooking tasks from wherever they are using an application on their phones, allowing them to come home to an already prepared meal.

Everybody needs to eat, but not everybody is a great chef. While food delivery services do exist, they are hardly economically or environmentally friendly in comparison to cooking at home. Another advantage of home cooking is that you know what is going into your food and can ensure you use healthy ingredients are used. Though there are many advantages to home cooking, it is also time consuming and depending on the desired dish there can be a significant skill level required. By providing assistance and automation, the CAS will make home cooking easier and allow for the menial tasks to be performed automatically, giving users more free time for work and recreation.

The CAS would require a fully integrated system to allow for each component to sync between each other and the user’s requests. Voice recognition would be required for ease of access to information and recipes. Cloud based storage would allow the ACS to store and sync favourite recipes and information. Robotic arms with pre-configured movements for chopping and stirring would need to be developed. Visual recognition would be required for the automatic chopper. The system will need to communicate with the application over long ranges so it would need a constant wi-fi connection.

Extensive knowledge of programming the many features of the CAS such as automatic chopping using visual recognition and the automation of the oven and stovetop systems. All of these features must also synchronise with each other and be controllable with the mobile application. The application itself will require knowledge of app development and user centred design principles and heuristics to provide a usable and effective way of controlled the CAS remotely.

A successful outcome would result in the CAS improving the quality of life for users by helping less experienced cooks and automating tasks for simple but menial tasks in the cooking process. The features of the CAS will simplify the process of cooking as everything works in tandem to deliver a user-friendly experience that can be controlled locally and remotely from far away so users can go somewhere while their food is being prepared or begin the process before they even arrive at home. As a successful product it would be a great step in creating a fully automated home environment giving users more time to work on other projects or for recreational activities.
